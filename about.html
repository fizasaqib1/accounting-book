FinTechsy By Fiza, Urwa & Aroona

An academic accounting system for an imaginary IT services business

1. Business Background & Stakeholders

FinTechsy is an imaginary Information Technology (IT) services business created for academic purposes by Fiza, Urwa and Aroona. The business provides software development, web and mobile application development, IT maintenance, and cloud-based services.

This project completes the entire accounting cycle of the business for a single accounting period from January 1, 2025 to April 30, 2025. All transactions recorded relate strictly to this accounting period and follow the accrual basis of accounting.

Although the business is imaginary, the transactions are designed to reflect realistic operations of a small IT services firm.

Stakeholders

  • Owner / Management — performance & profitability analysis
  • Employees — job stability and salary assurance
  • Creditors & Banks — repayment capacity assessment
  • Clients — reliability for long-term IT projects
  • Tax Authorities — accurate income reporting
02

Accounting Equation & Types of Accounts

FinTechsy follows the fundamental accounting equation:

Assets = Liabilities + Equity

Types of Accounts
  • Assets: Cash, Accounts Receivable, Equipment
  • Liabilities: Accounts Payable, Accrued Expenses, Bank Loan
  • Equity: Owner Capital
  • Revenue: Service Revenue, Maintenance Revenue
  • Expenses: Salaries, Rent, Utilities, Marketing, Depreciation
Accounting Basis
  • Accrual Basis
  • Double-Entry System
  • Matching Principle
